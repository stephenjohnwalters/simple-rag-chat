# Acme Corp Company Handbook

Welcome to **Acme Corp**! This handbook covers our mission, values, and day-to-day expectations for every employee.

## 1. Mission Statement

> Build delightful gadgets that simplify everyday life.

## 2. Core Values

- Customer Obsession
- Craftsmanship
- Bias for Action
- Ownership & Accountability
- Playful Innovation

## 3. Working Hours

Standard hours are 09:00–17:30 local time, Monday–Friday. Flexible schedules can be arranged with your manager.

| Region | Core Hours |
|--------|------------|
| AMER   | 10:00–14:00 PST |
| EMEA   | 10:00–15:00 CET |
| APAC   | 10:00–14:00 SGT |

## 4. Paid Time Off

Employees accrue **20 days** of PTO per calendar year plus local public holidays.

## 5. Code of Conduct

We expect respect, inclusion, and professionalism in all interactions.
