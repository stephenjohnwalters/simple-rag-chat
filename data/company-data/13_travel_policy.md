# Travel Policy

Effective: 2025-01-01

## Purpose

Ensure safe, cost-effective travel for customer visits and company events.

## Booking Guidelines

- Use **TripActions** for flights & hotels.
- Book ≥ 14 days in advance when possible.
- Economy for flights <6 h, premium economy 6–10 h, business >10 h.

## Daily Allowance

| Region | Meals | Local Transport |
|--------|-------|-----------------|
| US     | $60   | $30 |
| EU     | €55   | €25 |
| APAC   | S$80  | S$40 |

Receipts > $25 required. Alcohol non-reimbursable.

## Safety

Enroll in **International SOS** prior to departure.
