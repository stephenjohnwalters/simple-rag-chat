# Data Privacy Policy

Compliant with **GDPR**, **CCPA**, and **LGPD**.

## Personal Data We Collect

- Name, email, contact details
- Usage analytics (anonymized after 90 days)
- Billing information (stored with Stripe)

## Data Subject Rights

1. Access
2. Rectification
3. Erasure
4. Data portability

Submit requests via **privacy@acme.example**. We respond within 30 days.

| Data Type      | Retention |
|----------------|-----------|
| Logs           | 1 year |
| Customer contracts | 7 years |
| Marketing leads | 18 months |
