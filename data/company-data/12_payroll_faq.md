# Payroll FAQ

**Q:** When is payday?

**A:** The 15th and last business day of each month.

**Q:** How do I update my bank info?

**A:** Submit changes in the BambooHR portal at least 5 days before payroll cutoff.

**Q:** Where can I view my payslips?

> Dashboard → "Documents" → "Payslips".

Tax forms (W-2, 1099) are released by **January 31** of the following year.
